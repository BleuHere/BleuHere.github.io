ABOUT ME

Bleuette Hille is a Web Development Student at Enspiral DevAcademy.

Outside of the classroom you will find Bleuette cursing at her sewing machine, using her blunt kitchen knives to make delicious meals and walking her small, fancy dog, Alonso.

Her goal is to combat social and environmental issues through building useful tech alongside those who need it most. She believes that meaningful change happens when inclusion, communication and kindness are central values.

Bleuette manages to stay idealistic though her cynicism grows with dystopian Supreme Court decisions and people being rude to waitstaff.
